Early Mornings and Late Nights are My Best Friends

After starting my coding journey, I initially focused on Swift but struggled to find opportunities. After hearing about a bootcamp from friends, my husband and I decided I should join the C# Full Stack bootcamp, where I could apply the logic and knowledge I already had. My day usually starts before sunrise; I've found that the early hours, when my toddler is still asleep, are the best time for uninterrupted coding. In the evenings, my husband takes care of our child, giving me the time I need to complete labs and assessments.

A Land of Rich Heritage and Culture

Dindigul-Tamil Nadu, my homeland, places a strong emphasis on education and learning. Growing up in an environment that values knowledge and innovation encouraged me to pursue my academic interests diligently. Witnessing the growth and impact of technology in my home state motivated me to believe that with determination and hard work, I could carve out a successful career in IT, even while balancing the responsibilities of motherhood.
